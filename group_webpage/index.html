Our Mission Statement

We are a team of curious and driven computer science students committed to building meaningful, efficient, and creative solutions. Our strengths lie in our diverse skills, collaborative mindset, and shared passion for learning. We value clear communication, continuous improvement, and mutual respect. Our goal is to grow not only as developers but also as a supportive, problem-solving team.

Team Rules

  • Respect All Voices – Everyone’s ideas and perspectives are valid and worth hearing.
  • Communicate Openly & Frequently – Keep each other updated and ask for help when needed.
  • Meet Deadlines – Complete tasks on time to keep the project moving smoothly.
  • Support One Another – Offer help, share knowledge, and never blame—learn together.
  • Be Accountable – Take ownership of your tasks and follow through.
  • Stay Flexible – Be open to change and ready to adapt when plans shift.
